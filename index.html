How to play_

  1. Game has two players, they play by turns;
  2. Players roll the DICE to fire rounds to opponent - HOLD button stores opponents damages and shows them in general score;
  3. After clicking HOLD button its opponent turn;
  4. CURRENT box shows how many shells player can shot at once
    by rolling the dice;
  5. Player can roll the dice as many times he wishes;
  6. BUT if player rolls a 1, all his CURRENT rounds are lost, and it is opponents turn.
  7. Each player has chance to CRITICAL STRIKE that destroys opponent immediately; global damage/score have to be equal to: default settings - RoboCop: 13, 21, 44;
    Ed 209    : 33, 47;
  8. Players wins by damaging opponent. Default general score/damage is set to 50;
  9. Players can set winning sccore/damage manually by writing number in SET AMMO box; this operation changes default critical strikes settings as well.
